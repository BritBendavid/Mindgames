Digit Memory Timer

Auto-hide after your chosen interval • Wrong submit reveals the answer

Settings

The number will be shown and the timer will start. After your chosen interval, it will automatically hide (stopping the timer). You can still press Hide manually.

Controls

• Hide manually anytime (button).
• Submit with Enter/Done after it hides.
• If wrong, the correct answer is revealed and you can press Next.
