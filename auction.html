    How the Game Works

    Each player starts with $1000. The game lasts 15 rounds.

    In each round, every player has 60 seconds to secretly submit a bid. If a player does not submit in time, their bid counts as $0.

    When the timer ends, the player who bid the most wins the round. Every player loses the amount they bid, whether they won or not.

    If two or more players tie for the highest bid, those tied players enter sudden death. They get another 60 seconds to bid more money on top of what they already bid. Sudden death continues until one tied player bids more than the others.

    If tied players have the same amount of money left and all of it has been bid, the round is split between them.

    After 15 rounds, the player with the most round wins is the overall winner.
